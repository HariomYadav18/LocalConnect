Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our LocalConnect project

What is LocalConnect?

LocalConnect is a final year Computer Science project that demonstrates how local businesses can be connected with customers through a modern web platform. It's a proof-of-concept for local commerce digitalization.

Is this a real business?

No, this is an academic project developed by final year students. However, it demonstrates real-world functionality and could potentially be developed into a commercial platform.

Can I contribute to this project?

While this is primarily an academic project, we welcome feedback and suggestions. You can contact us through the form above or reach out to the development team directly.

What technologies were used?

The project uses HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and Tailwind CSS for the frontend. It demonstrates modern web development practices and responsive design principles.
