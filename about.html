About LocalConnect

A final year project by Hariom Yadav (VIT Bhopal University) exploring the potential of connecting local businesses with customers through innovative technology.

Our Story

LocalConnect was developed as a final year project by Hariom Yadav, a Computer Science student at VIT Bhopal University, who observed the challenges faced by local businesses in adapting to the digital age. The goal was to create a solution that could bridge the gap between traditional local commerce and modern e-commerce platforms.

Started in 2024 as an academic project for the 4th year B.Tech program, the project began with research and analysis of local business needs in the Bhopal area. The development process involved learning full-stack technologies, understanding user experience design, and implementing modern web development practices to create a comprehensive local business marketplace platform.

Project Objectives & Values

Our project is driven by a set of core objectives that guide our development approach

Community First

We believe in strengthening local communities by supporting neighborhood businesses and creating meaningful connections through our platform.

Innovation

We continuously explore innovative solutions to provide the best technology that makes local commerce seamless and efficient.

Trust & Quality

We maintain the highest standards of quality and trust, ensuring every transaction is secure and every product meets expectations.

Sustainability

We promote sustainable practices by reducing delivery distances and supporting local, eco-friendly businesses.

Inclusivity

We create opportunities for all types of local businesses, from small kirana stores to specialty shops.

Learning

We're committed to learning and improving our platform based on user feedback and real-world testing.
